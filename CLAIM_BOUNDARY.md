# Claim Boundary

This repository may be cited as a claim-bounded failure-boundary audit.

## Allowed claims

- A10-derived H0 scaffold materials were deconstructed and audited.
- Candidate proxy/scaffold material was retained as bounded hypothesis material.
- The audited chain did not establish a first-principles physical `E(z)`/`H(z)` bridge equation.
- Because that bridge was not established, Lambda-CDM reference smoke, real-data likelihood, MCMC, posterior comparison, and evidence evaluation were not executed.
- The result is a negative/diagnostic handoff, not a victory or defeat in observational cosmology.

## Forbidden claims

- A10 solved the Hubble tension.
- A10 replaced Lambda-CDM.
- Lambda-CDM was falsified or defeated.
- A10 was supported by real-data likelihood or posterior evidence.
- A physical `E(z)`/`H(z)` equation was derived.
- SN/BAO/CMB likelihood testing was completed.
- The audit establishes a new observational cosmology result.
