# Claim Boundary

This repository may be cited only as a claim-bounded failure-boundary audit and research-governance archive.

## Allowed claims

- A10-derived H0 scaffold materials were deconstructed and audited.
- Candidate proxy/scaffold material was retained as bounded hypothesis/handoff material.
- The audited chain did not establish a first-principles physical `E(z)` / `H(z)` bridge equation.
- Because that bridge was not established, Lambda-CDM / ΛCDM reference smoke, real-data likelihood, MCMC, posterior comparison, evidence evaluation, Bayes-factor, AIC, or BIC comparison were not executed.
- The result is a negative diagnostic closeout and failure-localization record, not a victory or defeat in observational cosmology.

## Forbidden claims

- A10 solved the Hubble tension.
- A10 replaced, defeated, or falsified Lambda-CDM / ΛCDM.
- A10 was supported by real-data likelihood, posterior evidence, Bayes factor, AIC, or BIC comparison.
- A physical `E(z)` / `H(z)` equation was derived.
- SN/BAO/CMB likelihood testing was completed.
- The audit establishes a new observational cosmology result.
- The retained scaffold is a deployable cosmological model.
- This archive is a peer-reviewed expert cosmology conclusion.

## Required reuse statement

Any derivative summary should preserve the following boundary or an equivalent statement:

> This archive documents a claim-bounded failure-boundary audit. It retained candidate scaffold material but did not establish a first-principles physical `E(z)` / `H(z)` bridge. Therefore it does not report a real-data likelihood, Lambda-CDM / ΛCDM comparison, MCMC posterior, evidence metric, Hubble-tension solution, or Lambda-CDM / ΛCDM replacement claim.
