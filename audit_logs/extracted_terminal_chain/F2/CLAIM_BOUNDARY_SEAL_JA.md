# Claim Boundary Seal — Mode F2

## Allowed claims

- A symbolic bridge contract candidate was tested on a synthetic nonnegative redshift grid.
- The candidate passed finite/positive/normalization/inverse/integral/domain/leakage guards in this toy setting.
- The result is useful for failure-localization and bridge-scaffold triage.

## Disallowed claims

- A physical `E(z)` or `H(z)` equation has been derived.
- A cosmological observable distance has been computed.
- A10 has been compared with ΛCDM.
- A10 improves, solves, or explains the Hubble tension.
- A10 replaces ΛCDM.
- Any SN/BAO/CMB likelihood, MCMC, posterior, evidence, AIC/BIC, or Bayes factor has been evaluated.
