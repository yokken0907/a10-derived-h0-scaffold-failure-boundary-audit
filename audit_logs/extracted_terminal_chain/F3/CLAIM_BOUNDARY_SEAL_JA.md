- F3 classifies the post-F2 candidate as a symbolic scaffold with explicit derivation gaps.

- The candidate is a validated physical `E(z)` or `H(z)`.
- The candidate has been compared with ΛCDM.
- Any posterior, evidence, Bayes factor, chi-square, AIC, or BIC result exists.
